Core Thesis

The Future of AI Coding:
Not Just Coding, But Idea-to-Execution.

This is my product thesis — and the biggest opportunity of this era.

1Current AI Coding products are targeting the wrong goal

Most AI Coding products come from程序员 backgrounds, speak programmers' language, and target programmers as users. That's fine — but it's a starting point, not the终点. The real growth lies in a much larger group: PMs, designers, marketers, sales, content creators, entrepreneurs... Everyone who has ideas but can't write code.

2Data proves it: 10x user gap

Claude Code has ~200K users — all developers. When Anthropic launched Claude for broader users (cowork mode), user base hit 2 million. That's a 10x gap. This isn't coincidence. The market is telling us: idea executors far outnumber code writers — and they want tools more.

3The real mission: turning ideas into products

ChatGPT helps me express ideas as content. AI coding tools help me realize ideas as products. This is a fundamental shift. Products that can do this will break the "programmers only" ceiling and reach a market 10x to 100x larger.
